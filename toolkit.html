Job Fair Toolkit

Scripts and tools for the booth. Keep it short. Keep it human.

What We Are
"Servi is a freelancer marketplace for family services in Lebanon. Families can't find reliable freelancers, and people with real skills have no structured channel to be found. Many don't even know the opportunity exists. We built the bridge: identified talents, verified profiles, app-based bookings, payment secured... And this is where YOU come in. We're pre-launch, building this right now, and we want the right freelancers to build it with us. The people who join today aren't just our supply, they're the ones shaping what Servi becomes."
